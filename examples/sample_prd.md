# Subscription Alerts V1

## Goal

Help users subscribe to updates for a company profile and receive alerts when a
new risk event appears.

## User Stories

- As a user, I can follow a company from the company profile page.
- As a user, I can view all followed companies in one list.
- As a user, I receive an alert when a followed company has a new legal,
  compliance, or ownership event.

## Requirements

- Add a Follow button to the company profile page.
- Store followed companies per signed-in user.
- Send an email notification for new events.
- Let users unfollow a company.

## Open Questions

- Notification frequency is not specified.
- The PRD does not say what happens when email delivery fails.
